Vegan banana muffins

Moist and yummy vegan banana muffins

Ingredients

  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 mashed ripe bananas
  • 1 cup coconut milk

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease 12 muffin cups or line with paper liners.
  2. Mix flour, sugar, cinnamon, baking powder and salt together in a large bowl. Stir bananas and coconut milk together in a separate bowl; stir banana mixture into flour mixture until just combined. Divide batter among the muffin cups, filling each about 3/4 full.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean, 30 to 35 minutes.
