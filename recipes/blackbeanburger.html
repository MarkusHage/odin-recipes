Vegan black bean burger

Scrumdiddlyumptious vegan black bean burger

Ingredients

  • 1 can black beans
  • 3 baby carrots, grated
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp warm water
  • Flour as needed
  • various spices of your liking

Steps

  1. Preheat a pan to medium-high heat
  2. Mash black beans in a bowl. Add carrots, onion and garlic; mix well.
  3. Whisk cornstarch, water and spices together in a separate small bowl
  4. Add the water and spices to the bean mixture
  5. Stir and add flour as needed until a sticky batter forms
  6. Shape the batter into burgers
  7. Fry them in the pan until they ger crispy
  8. Serve in a hamburger bun
